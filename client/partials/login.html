Bellator 154: Davis vs. King Mo

Photo by: Dave Mandel

Photo by: Dave Mandel

Phil Davis is now officially Bellator MMA’s light heavyweight Number 1 contender, following his win against King Mo at Bellator 154.

The UFC veteran took a unanimous decision over former Strikeforce titlist Muhammed Lawal (King Mo) at SAP Center in our hometown San Jose, California!

Two judges submitted 30-27 scorecards for Davis, while a third saw it 29-28, also for “Mr. Wonderful.” It was clear Davis was faster, more conditioned and agressive

during their bout. Especially, after Davis slick combo take down almost ending in a TKO. Davis' win sets him up for a fight with reigning 205-pound cham pLiam McGeary.

Is Davis going to continue to smash through the Bellator division or will he meet the same fate as Benson Henderson?
